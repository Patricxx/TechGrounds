About Me...



Design is design, as long there is inspiration, there is a way to create it! Design includes everything, from graphic, branding, fashion, app, ui/ux, visual, industrial, furniture, interior untill architectural design!


I’am a Multi-disciplinary designer (INFJ-A/INFJ-T) with 20 years of extensive experience in graphic design, also furniture, interior design, and up-comming to ad Front-end-Developing and later UI&UX experiences to this list.


My passion and love for design, art, furniture, interior, architecture and philosophy goes deep. I get my inspiration from the most strangest to the most common things. Every new project is a new challenge on its own and there is a tailored solutions for every problem. And every day I try to approach live with a positive attitude an open mind.


Since I've been working in the design field I have developed myself in the broad sence of design but I’am always open for new ideas and visions of others. Thats why I love to collaborate with others to create beautiful designs and bring the design to a higher level.
